About Prediction

Method to calculate the RUL from the result and graph obtained. Pass the test data till current time index through the architecture & store the predicted fault probabilty values. Then fit the predicted fault probability values and corresponding time indices through a regressor to extrapolate and find the time index when the fault probability exceeds a predefined threshold to consider occurrence of failure. Here in the resultant graph Linear regression & Gaussian Process Regression (GPR) provided by sklearn pythong tool baris used to get the results in graphic form and the fault probability threshold is set at 0.9. It can vary as per choice.
